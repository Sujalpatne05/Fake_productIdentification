    How Verification Works

    Each product registered on the blockchain has a unique serial number. When you scan a QR code or enter the SN, the smart contract checks if the product was legitimately transferred through the authorized chain (Manufacturer → Seller → You). If any link is missing or fraudulent, the product is flagged as counterfeit.
